Features we offer

Here are the feature that we offer

Dashboard

An interactive tool that aggregates and displays data on reported crimes, showing locations, types of incidents, and trends to help authorities and the public monitor and respond to crime patterns efficiently

Crime Pinpoint

Involves identifying and reporting the exact location and details of a crime, including time, nature of the incident, and any evidence, to help authorities target their response accurately.

Report Crime

Reporting a crime involves notifying authorities with clear, detailed information about the incident, including time, place, suspects, and evidence, to help them take appropriate action.
